# P05 OBD1 ECU (1992–1995 Civic CX D15B8)

## Overview

The P05 is an OBD1 engine control unit designed for the 1992–1995 Honda Civic CX equipped with the D15B8 SOHC non-VTEC engine. This ECU variant features:

- **Engine:** D15B8 SOHC (single overhead cam), non-VTEC
- **Emissions:** Integrated catalytic converter with single-wire O2 sensor
- **Platform:** OBD1 diagnostic standard
- **Application:** Fuel-efficient compact engine control

The P05 represents an OBD1 evolution of the earlier 1988–1991 HF (high-fuel-efficiency) motor series and serves as a foundation for tuning and engine conversion projects.

> [!NOTE]
> This ECU is commonly used in conversion and tuning applications due to its compatibility with non-VTEC D-series engines and straightforward OBD1 architecture.
