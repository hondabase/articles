# P28-G03 ECU Technical Reference

The P28-G03 is an OBDII-compliant ECU utilized in European 1996+ Honda del Sol models equipped with SOHC VTEC engines. 

> [!NOTE]
> The hardware architecture and internal layout of the P28-G03 share significant similarities with the P73 ECU series.

## Overview
This unit is designed for the European market and manages SOHC VTEC engine operations under OBDII protocols. 

### Key Specifications
*   **Market:** Europe
*   **OBD Standard:** OBDII
*   **Engine Compatibility:** SOHC VTEC
*   **Chassis Compatibility:** del Sol (1996+)

## Hardware Comparison
The P28-G03 board layout mirrors the P73 architecture. When performing diagnostics or modifications, refer to P73-specific pinouts and component traces if direct P28-G03 documentation is unavailable.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Ensure that any diagnostic equipment used is compatible with the European OBDII protocol, as pinouts may differ from North American OBDII standards.
