# P70 ECU Specifications

The P70 ECU is an OBD1 engine control unit utilized in 1992–1995 Honda Domani vehicles equipped with the SOHC VTEC ZC engine.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This ECU variant does not support the oil pressure sensor input typically found in other OBD1 configurations.

## Overview
The P70 is designed for specific regional market requirements. It manages the SOHC VTEC ZC engine platform while omitting specific diagnostic inputs required by other OBD1 Honda ECUs.

## Technical Details
*   **OBD Standard:** OBD1
*   **Target Engine:** SOHC VTEC ZC
*   **Vehicle Application:** 1992–1995 Honda Domani
*   **Notable Omission:** No oil pressure sensor monitoring circuitry.

## Compatibility
This ECU is part of the standard OBD1 architecture. For wiring reference and pinout verification, refer to the standard OBD1 ECU pinout documentation.
