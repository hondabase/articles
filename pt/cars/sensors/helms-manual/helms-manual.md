# Honda and Acura Service Manuals (Helm Inc.)

Helm Inc. is the authorized publisher and distributor of official Honda and Acura service manuals. These manuals provide comprehensive technical specifications, wiring diagrams, and diagnostic procedures required for vehicle maintenance and repair.

## Supported Chassis Generations

The following chassis generations are supported by official Helm service documentation:

*   **EF:** 1988–1991 Civic / CRX
*   **EG:** 1992–1995 Civic
*   **DA:** 1990–1993 Integra
*   **DC:** 1994–2001 Integra

> [!NOTE]
> Always refer to the specific service manual for your chassis and engine code to ensure accurate torque specifications, pinout diagrams, and troubleshooting steps.
