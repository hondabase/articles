# Honda PW1 ECU Technical Reference

The PW1 ECU is a factory engine control unit utilized in 1990s Honda Concerto vehicles equipped with the D15B2 engine.

> [!NOTE]
> This ECU operates on the OBD0 platform. Ensure compatibility with your specific wiring harness and engine sensor configuration before installation.

## Overview
The PW1 is designed to manage the fuel injection and ignition timing for the D15B2 engine. It is a standard component for this chassis and engine combination.

## Technical Specifications
*   **Platform:** OBD0
*   **Engine Application:** D15B2
*   **Vehicle Application:** Honda Concerto (1993)
