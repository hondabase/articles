# P29 OBD1 ECU Technical Reference

The P29 ECU is an OBD1 engine control unit utilized in Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) Honda Domani and Civic 25th Anniversary models. It is specifically calibrated for the DOHC ZC engine platform.

## Overview
The P29 shares the standard OBD1 architecture common to the 1992–1995 Honda Civic/Integra chassis. It is frequently sought after for its specific fuel and ignition maps tailored to the DOHC ZC engine.

> [!NOTE]
> Ensure your engine harness matches the standard OBD1 pinout configuration before installation.

## ECU Specifications
*   **Generation:** OBD1
*   **Market:** JDM (Japanese Domestic Market)
*   **Engine Application:** DOHC ZC
*   **Chassis Application:** Domani / Civic 25th Anniversary

## Compatibility
The P29 is physically and electrically compatible with standard 1992–1995 Honda OBD1 engine harnesses. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> While the ECU is plug-and-play for most OBD1 chassis, verify that your specific engine sensor suite (e.g., O2 sensor type, knock sensor presence) matches the requirements of the P29 calibration to avoid Check Engine Light (CEL) triggers.
