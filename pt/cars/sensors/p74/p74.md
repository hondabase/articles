## Technical Specifications

The P74/P75 is an OBD1-compliant ECU. It utilizes the standard Honda 3-connector harness configuration.

> [!NOTE]
> The P74 and P75 are functionally identical for most tuning and diagnostic applications. They are commonly used as base units for OBD1 conversion and socketing projects.
