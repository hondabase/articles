# Sourcing Components via Future Active

[Future Active](http://www.future-active.com) is a global distributor of electronic components. They are a reliable source for the following hardware required for ECU chip tuning:

*   **SST 27SF256:** Flash memory chips compatible with OBD1 and OBD2 ECU modifications.
*   **Aries Low-Profile ZIF Sockets:** Zero Insertion Force sockets used to facilitate easy chip swapping during the tuning process.

> [!TIP]
> When ordering ZIF sockets, ensure you select the low-profile variant to maintain clearance within the ECU housing.
