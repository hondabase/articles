# C2785 NPN Switching Transistor Specifications

The C2785 is a high-speed NPN switching transistor commonly utilized in Honda ECU circuitry. It is functionally interchangeable with the C144 transistor in most ECU applications.

## Compatible Replacements

The following components are verified substitutes for the C2785:

| Part Number | Manufacturer/Source | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NTE2361** | NTE Electronics | Direct replacement |
| **2SD1423** | Panasonic | Available via DigiKey (Part: 2SD1423ARACT-ND) |
| **C2458Y** | Toshiba | Common alternative |

> [!TIP]
> For detailed electrical characteristics, pinout diagrams, and thermal ratings, refer to the [NTE2361 Datasheet](http://www.nteinc.com/specs/2300to2399/pdf/nte2361.pdf).

## Application Notes

*   **Compatibility:** These transistors are suitable for use in OBD0, OBD1, and OBD2 Honda ECU hardware.
*   **Handling:** Ensure proper ESD precautions are taken when soldering these components to the ECU PCB to prevent damage to the semiconductor junctions.
