# DigiKey Electronic Component Sourcing

DigiKey is a primary global distributor for electronic components, widely used for sourcing parts required for ECU repair, hardware modification, and custom sensor integration.

## Overview
DigiKey provides an extensive inventory of semiconductors, passive components, and connectors. Their platform is the industry standard for locating specific part numbers required for Honda ECU hardware projects, such as:

*   **Capacitors:** High-temperature electrolytic capacitors for ECU recapping.
*   **Resistors:** Precision resistors for signal conditioning and sensor scaling.
*   **Transistors/ICs:** Replacement logic gates, drivers, and microcontrollers.
*   **Connectors:** OEM-style headers and pins for wiring harnesses.

> [!TIP]
> When searching for components, use the manufacturer's part number (found on the component casing or in technical schematics) rather than generic descriptions to ensure exact compatibility.

## Procurement Tips
*   **Datasheets:** Always download the manufacturer's datasheet from the product page to verify electrical characteristics before ordering.
*   **Minimum Order Quantities:** Be aware that some specialized components may have minimum order quantities (MOQ) or reel requirements.
*   **Shipping:** DigiKey offers various shipping methods; for urgent repairs, expedited shipping is available to most regions.

## Common Components
For standard ECU maintenance, the following categories are frequently referenced:

| Component Type | Application |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Electrolytic Capacitors** | ECU power supply filtering and aging component replacement. |
| **Header Pins** | ECU connector repair and daughterboard installation. |
