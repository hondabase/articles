# C144 NPN Switching Transistor

The C144 is an NPN switching transistor commonly utilized in Honda ECU circuitry. 

## Replacement Information

When the C144 is unavailable or requires replacement during ECU repair, the **C2785** is a verified functional equivalent.

> [!TIP]
> When sourcing replacements for the C2785, ensure the substitute transistor matches the original NPN switching characteristics and package footprint required for the specific PCB location.
