# Wiki Wiki Web

A [Wiki Wiki Web](/cars/rom/wiki-wiki-web) is a site where everyone can collaborate on the content. The most well-known and widely used Wiki is the Portland Pattern Repository at [http://c2.com/cgi-bin/wiki?WikiWikiWeb](http://c2.com/cgi-bin/wiki?WikiWikiWeb). You and I will find these statements there particularly relevant: ''The point is to make the [Edit Text](/cars/rom/edit-text) form simple and the [Find Page](/cars/diagnostics/find-page) search fast.'' ''In addition to being quick, this site also aspires to Zen ideals generally labeled [Wabi Sabi](/cars/sensors/wabi-sabi). Zen finds beauty in the imperfect and ephemeral. When it comes down to it, that's all you need.'' ''The Universe tends toward chaos. The Universe tends toward Wiki.'' You can say hello on [Recent Visitors](/cars/sensors/recent-visitors), or read about [How To Use Wiki](/cars/diagnostics/how-to-use-wiki) and [Adding Pages](/cars/rom/adding-pages). Currently this Wiki has no theme for discussion, so go ahead and play with it and have fun!! --SteveWainstead.
