# PJ0

The A20A 86-89 Accord/Prelude PJ0 [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) had many revisions in different countries. Some of them will lack an O2 sensor or EGR control. However, they all use a vacuum advance distributor.

Most those [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s were made by Matsushita. Some of them (86-87?)use an external 128k [ROM](/cars/rom/rom).

The California Emissions PJ0 were OKI `80C154` based and always use a 128k External [ROM](/cars/rom/rom)
