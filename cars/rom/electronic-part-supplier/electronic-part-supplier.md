# Electronic Part Supplier Reference

This guide provides a list of reputable electronic component suppliers and specific part references for ECU hardware modification and repair.

## Recommended Suppliers

*   **DigiKey:** Excellent in-stock availability, comprehensive catalog, and user-friendly website.
*   **Arrow:** High in-stock availability; frequently carries AT29C256 Flash PROMs.
*   **Newark:** Reliable, professional, and fast shipping services.
*   **Moates.net:** Specialized supplier for automotive tuning hardware, including ZIF sockets and EEPROM chips.
*   **USBid:** Recommended for sourcing obsolete or hard-to-find components.
*   **Allied Electronics:** Useful for general electronic components, though availability for specific automotive-grade chips may vary.
*   **ProgShop:** Germany-based supplier; a viable resource for international sourcing of EPROMs.
*   **All American Semiconductor:** Alternative source for AT29C256 chips (note: typically requires a minimum order).
