# Electronic Part Supplier

a handful of major electronic part suppliers:

- [Digikey](http://web.archive.org/web/20260612163410/https://www.digikey.com/) - good in-stock availability, great catalog, good website.
- [Arrow](https://www.arrow.com) - GREAT in-stock availability, and they now carry AT29C256 Flash PROMs, $5 ea, no minimum. (12/03)
- [All American Semiconductor](http://web.archive.org/web/20161005153313/http://www.allamerican.com:80/) - Good place for AT[29 C256](/cars/diagnostics/29c256) but $50 minimum order.
- [Newark](http://www.newark.com) - fast, professional shipping
- [NuHorizons](http://web.archive.org/web/20130618003737/http://nuhorizons.com/) - I ordered the groupbuy of 500 SI-5151SG [515 X High Side Switch](/cars/rom/515x-high-side-switch) es from them.
- [MOATES. NET](http://www.moates.net) - from DIY-EFI. ORG Craig offers lots of help and great support. $6 `29C256`, $7 lowpro [ZIF](/cars/rom/zif), $6 shipping.
- [ProgShop](http://web.archive.org/web/20050211123612/http://progshop.com:80/shop/eproms/) - Germany location, so shipping is expensive to U. S., but it's another resource...
