# Fuel Cut

A Fuel Cut is when fuel to the motor is shut off completely. This is usually done as part of a [Rev Limiter](/cars/sensors/rev-limiter), [Launch Control](/cars/rom/launch-control) or [Boost Cut](/cars/rom/boost-cut) routine. [Fuel Cut](/cars/rom/fuel-cut) [Rev Limiter](/cars/sensors/rev-limiter)s are thought to be somewhat scary, as the engine may run lean from lack of fuel. [Spark Cut](/cars/sensors/spark-cut) is not performed by the stock [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu), but many aftermarket ignitions use it. [Spark Cut](/cars/sensors/spark-cut) [Rev Limiter](/cars/sensors/rev-limiter)s are thought to be somewhat less harsh.
