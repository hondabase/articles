# VTEC Crossover Point

In a [VTEC](/cars/sensors/vtec) motor, the [VTEC Crossover Point](/cars/rom/vtec-crossover-point) is the [RPM](/cars/sensors/rpm) at which the [VTEC Solenoid](/cars/wiring/vtec-solenoid) is activated in order to switch from the [Low Cam](/cars/sensors/low-cam) to the [High Cam](/cars/sensors/high-cam).
