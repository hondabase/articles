# EPROM

An EPROM is programmable [ROM](/cars/rom/rom) that can be written using a [ROM burner](/cars/ecu/rom-burner).

The [27C256](/cars/ecu/27c256) and [29C256](/cars/diagnostics/29c256) are the most common types that are pin-compatible with [OBD0](/cars/rom/obd0) and [OBD1](/cars/wiring/obd1) Honda [ECUs](/cars/ecu/ecu). The [28C256](/cars/ecu/28c256) is not pin-compatible.
