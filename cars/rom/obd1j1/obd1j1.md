# OBD1J1

`J1` on [OBD1](/cars/wiring/obd1) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s controls the state of [_EA](/cars/rom/ea). It is used to change whether the [MCU](/cars/rom/mcu) uses an external [ROM](/cars/rom/rom) or internal program.
