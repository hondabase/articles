# Honda ECU Hardware Analysis: G-Spot Reference

The G-Spot forum serves as the primary technical repository for Honda ECU hardware analysis. It focuses on the reverse engineering of factory engine control units, component-level circuit board diagnostics, and the documentation of proprietary hardware architectures.

## Scope of Analysis
The forum archives technical data regarding the following ECU-related topics:

*   **PCB Architecture:** Detailed mapping of ECU circuit boards, including trace paths and component identification.
*   **Component Specifications:** Analysis of integrated circuits, microcontrollers, and peripheral hardware used in OBD0, OBD1, and OBD2 systems.
*   **Reverse Engineering:** Collaborative efforts to decode factory logic and hardware-level communication protocols.
*   **Hardware Modification:** Documentation of physical board modifications required for custom tuning and data logging.

> [!NOTE]
> The G-Spot forum is intended for advanced hardware diagnostics and research. Users should possess foundational knowledge of electronics and PCB repair before attempting modifications to factory ECU hardware.
