# Acura Brand History and Overview

Acura was established in 1986 as the luxury division of Honda. The brand's inaugural luxury vehicle, the Acura Legend, received the *Car and Driver* Import Car of the Year award in 1991. 

Following the success of the Legend, Acura expanded its lineup to include performance-oriented models such as the Integra and the flagship NSX. The NSX was notably recognized as the "Best Sports Car Ever Built" by *Car and Driver* in 1990.

## Current and Legacy Lineup
Acura's historical and modern performance portfolio includes:

*   **NSX:** High-end sports car.
*   **Integra:** Performance compact.
*   **RSX:** Sport coupe.
*   **TSX:** Sport sedan.
*   **TL:** Executive sedan.
