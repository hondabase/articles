# Atmel AT29C010 Flash ROM

The Atmel AT29C010 is a 1Mbit (128kb) FLASH-ROM chip. These were commonly found on older computer motherboards from the Pentium I and Pentium II era.

---

## ECU Implementation

While this chip can be used in Honda ECUs, it is **not a drop-in replacement**. Due to pinout differences compared to standard [27C256](/cars/ecu/27c256) or [29C256](/cars/diagnostics/29c256) chips, hardware alterations or wiring adapters are required to make this chip functional in an ECU.

### Programming Considerations
When burning data to this chip, you must ensure the start address corresponds to the address space the ECU expects for its ROM map. Incorrect addressing will result in the ECU failing to boot the firmware.
