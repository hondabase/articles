# 29C010

The [ATMEL](http://web.archive.org/web/20171230124942/http://www.atmel.com/) AT29C010 chip is a [FLASH](/cars/rom/flash)-[ROM](/cars/rom/rom) chip with 1Mbit(~128kb) of memory. It can be found in old Pentium I&II computer motherbords. This chip will work in [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s, but require pins to be moved around/altered due to them being a different pinout than either [27 C256](/cars/ecu/27c256) or [29 C256](/cars/diagnostics/29c256) chips. When writing/burning the program, you need to specify the start address, where your [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) looks at. [Datasheet for an Atmel 29C010 (PDF)](http://www.futurlec.com/Datasheet/Memory/AT29C010.pdf)
