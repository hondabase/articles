# Spark Plug Technical Reference

The **spark plug** is the critical ignition component that receives electrical energy from the [Ignition Coil](/cars/ignition/ignition-coil) and discharges it across a small gap. This arc ignites the compressed air-fuel mixture within the [Combustion Chamber](/cars/wiring/combustion-chamber).

---

## Functionality
*   **Ignition:** The spark plug initiates the combustion process.
*   **Heat Range:** Plugs are rated by their "heat range," which refers to their ability to dissipate heat from the firing tip into the cylinder head. Selecting the appropriate heat range is vital for preventing [Pre-Ignition](/cars/diagnostics/pre-ignition) in high-performance or forced-induction engines.

*Maintaining properly gapped and clean spark plugs is essential for consistent ignition performance and engine longevity.*
