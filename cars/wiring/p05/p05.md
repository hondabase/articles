# P05 OBD1 ECU Reference

The **P05** is a 92-95 OBD1 ECU originally equipped in the Honda Civic CX (D15B8 engine). It is notable for its fuel-economy-focused design, featuring a close-coupled catalytic converter and 1-wire O2 sensor configuration, making it the OBD1 successor to the 88-91 HF engine management system.

---

## Technical Specifications
*   **Engine:** D15B8 (SOHC non-VTEC)
*   **Application:** 1992–1995 Civic CX
*   **Emissions:** Designed for close-coupled catalytic converter operation.
*   **O2 Sensor:** 1-wire configuration.

*This ECU is primarily sought after for its fuel-efficient calibration, though it is less common for high-performance tuning compared to VTEC-capable ECUs like the P28.*
