# A/C Thermostat Switch Bypass

If you need to bypass the A/C thermostat switch, follow these steps to jump the appropriate connections.

---

## Under-Dash Procedure

Locate the 2-pin connector wrapped in blue tape, situated near the heater blower unit under the dashboard.

*   **Wiring Colors:** Blue/Red and Yellow/White.

## Under-Hood Procedure

Locate the A/C sub-harness connector on the passenger side of the engine bay. Jump the Blue/Red wire to the Yellow/White wire to complete the circuit.

---

*Note: This procedure is intended for troubleshooting purposes or during specific A/C installation scenarios.*
