# RM11

Author: Deluded Date: 04-04-03 01:19 RM11 CAN be replaced with 3 resistors: From pin 1 to 2...10k ohm (digikey# 10kebk-nd (200 for US$4.70)) 2 to 3...51K (51kebk-nd) 3 to 8...12K (12kebk-nd) No MIL and normal operation unplug VTEC sol and MIL functions correctly by throwing [CEL](/cars/wiring/cel). I'm not smart, just observant and persistent: look closely at a P72 if you want to see where I came up with this. Editors note: This RM11 replacement will probably NOT work for auto [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s if the feedback on the auto trans lockup solenoid is required. A true RM11 or additional resistors would be required to run both a VTEC and auto trans solenoid with feedback. I removed an RM11 and measured all the resistances.
