# C144 NPN Switching Transistor

The C144 is an NPN switching transistor commonly utilized within Honda ECU circuitry for signal switching and logic control.

## Replacement Components

When the original C144 component is unavailable or requires replacement, the following transistor is a verified substitute:

*   **C2785:** This component serves as a direct functional replacement for the C144.

> [!NOTE]
> Ensure that the pinout orientation of the replacement transistor matches the PCB footprint of the original C144 before soldering.
