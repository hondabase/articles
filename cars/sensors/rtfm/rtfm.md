# Technical Documentation and Resource Guidelines

Before initiating modifications or troubleshooting procedures on Honda engine management systems, it is essential to consult the available technical documentation. Most technical inquiries regarding ECU architecture, sensor calibration, and wiring have been thoroughly documented in existing service manuals and technical archives.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always prioritize official service manuals and verified technical databases before attempting hardware modifications. Utilizing established resources ensures safety and prevents irreversible damage to the ECU or vehicle wiring harness.

## Recommended Research Workflow

To resolve technical issues efficiently, follow this standard research procedure:

1.  **Consult the Service Manual:** Review the factory service manual (FSM) for the specific chassis and engine combination.
2.  **Search Technical Archives:** Utilize the Hondabase search functionality to locate existing documentation on specific ECU pinouts, sensor scaling, or diagnostic procedures.
3.  **Review Component Specifications:** Verify all electrical values, wire colors, and signal types against the provided technical tables before making physical connections.
