This is one of Dave B's pet projects. The stock honda knock board leaves a lot to be desired. It would be nice to incorporate a more modern stategy for preignition detection. **Candidates:**

- DSP-based spectral analysis
- Genetic algorithm based spectral analysis
- Ion sensing
