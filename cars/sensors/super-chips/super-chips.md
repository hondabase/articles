# Super Chips Automotive Tuning Overview

Super Chips is a global automotive performance company specializing in electronic control unit (ECU) recalibration and chiptuning across a wide range of vehicle manufacturers.

## Overview
The company provides aftermarket performance solutions designed to optimize engine management parameters. By modifying the factory-programmed fuel and ignition maps stored within the ECU, Super Chips enables adjustments to engine performance characteristics.

> [!NOTE]
> While Super Chips offers broad support for various automotive brands, specific compatibility with Honda/Acura OBD-series ECUs varies by model year and hardware revision.

## Core Tuning Functions
Super Chips tuning solutions typically focus on the following engine management areas:

*   **Ignition Timing:** Adjusting spark advance curves to optimize combustion efficiency.
*   **Fuel Mapping:** Modifying air-fuel ratios to improve power delivery or throttle response.
*   **Rev Limiter Adjustments:** Raising or lowering the maximum engine speed threshold.
*   **Speed Limiter Removal:** Adjusting or removing factory-set top speed governors.

## Compatibility
Super Chips products are engineered to interface with factory ECU hardware. Users should verify specific hardware compatibility before attempting to flash or modify any Honda/Acura engine management system.

> [!WARNING]
> Always ensure the ECU hardware version matches the specific tuning file provided by the manufacturer. Flashing an incompatible calibration can result in a non-responsive ECU.
