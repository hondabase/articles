# `MAX233`

the [Maxim ](http://web.archive.org/web/20250125103331/http://www.maxim-ic.com./) `MAX233` chip is commonly used to translate between [TTL](/cars/sensors/ttl) and [RS232](/cars/sensors/rs232) levels during [serial communication](/cars/tuning/serial-communication). It does not require any external capacitors, unlike the `MAX232`. [Datasheet here](http://web.archive.org/web/20120105170102/http://pdfserv.maxim-ic.com:80/en/ds/MAX220-MAX249.pdf).
