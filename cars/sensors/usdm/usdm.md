# USDM

United States Domestic Market - cars destined/made for the United States. Compare to: [EDM](/cars/wiring/edm), [JDM](/cars/sensors/jdm)Note: [USDM](/cars/sensors/usdm) [OBD1 Civic Integra](/cars/sensors/obd1-civic-integra) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s generally are the most feature-filled [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s, and generally have the [Injector Test Circuit](/cars/diagnostics/injector-test-circuit), [Knock Sensor](/cars/ignition/knock-sensor) (in [DOHC](/cars/sensors/dohc) [VTEC](/cars/sensors/vtec) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s), [Electrical Load Detector](/cars/sensors/electrical-load-detector) and [Pressure Atmosphere](/cars/rom/pressure-atmosphere) sensors. They generally do not have surface mount components, and use a larger rectangular case compared to [JDM](/cars/sensors/jdm) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s. They also generally do not have a [Speed Limiter](/cars/diagnostics/speed-limiter).
