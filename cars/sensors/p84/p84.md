# P84 OBD1 ECU Specifications

The P84 is an OBD1 engine control unit (ECU) utilized in the Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) Honda Civic ETi. This ECU is specifically calibrated for the VTEC-E engine platform paired with an automatic transmission.

## Overview
The P84 ECU manages the lean-burn characteristics of the VTEC-E system, optimizing fuel economy during low-load operation. It is designed exclusively for automatic transmission configurations.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The P84 is hardware-specific to the VTEC-E engine. Using this ECU on non-VTEC-E engines or manual transmission vehicles may result in improper fuel mapping, transmission control errors, or check engine lights.
