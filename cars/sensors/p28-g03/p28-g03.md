# P28-G03 ECU Technical Reference

The P28-G03 is an OBDII-compliant Engine Control Unit (ECU) utilized in 1996 and later European Honda del Sol models equipped with SOHC VTEC engines. 

> [!NOTE]
> The physical architecture and board layout of the P28-G03 closely resemble the P73 ECU series.

## Specifications
*   **OBD Standard:** OBDII
*   **Market:** European (EU)
*   **Engine Compatibility:** SOHC VTEC
*   **Chassis Compatibility:** del Sol (EG)

## Hardware Overview
The P28-G03 utilizes an OBDII-style connector and board architecture. When performing diagnostics or hardware modifications, ensure compatibility with the specific pinout requirements of the European OBDII harness.

> [!CAUTION]
> While the board layout shares similarities with the P73, internal components and mapping may differ. Always verify specific component values before attempting cross-platform hardware swaps.
