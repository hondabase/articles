# DigiKey Electronic Component Sourcing

DigiKey is a primary supplier for electronic components required for ECU repair, hardware modification, and custom sensor integration. Their inventory includes a wide range of semiconductors, passive components, and connectors compatible with Honda engine management systems.

## Ordering Guidelines

When sourcing components for automotive electronics, ensure parts meet the necessary environmental and electrical specifications.

> [!TIP]
> Use the DigiKey part search to filter by specific parameters such as operating temperature range (automotive grade -40°C to +125°C is recommended) and package type (e.g., DIP, SOIC, or SMD).

## Common Component Categories

When searching for replacement parts for OBD-series ECUs, focus on the following categories:

*   **Capacitors:** Electrolytic capacitors for power supply filtering and signal smoothing.
*   **Resistors:** Precision resistors for sensor scaling and signal conditioning.
*   **Transistors/MOSFETs:** Replacement drivers for fuel injectors, ignition coils, and VTEC solenoids.
*   **Connectors:** OEM-style headers and pins for ECU harness repair or custom breakout boards.
