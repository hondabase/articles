# Keihin Indiana Precision Technology (IPT)

Keihin Indiana Precision Technology (IPT) is a primary manufacturer and design partner for Honda Engine Control Modules (ECM/PCM). 

## Manufacturing Overview

While Keihin is the primary supplier for the majority of Honda engine management systems, manufacturing responsibilities have historically been distributed among several specialized automotive electronics firms. 

> [!NOTE]
> Honda refers to the Engine Control Unit (ECU) as the Engine Control Module (ECM) or Powertrain Control Module (PCM) in official service documentation.

## Supplier Variations

Technical documentation and hardware teardowns indicate that while Keihin maintains the majority of the market share for Honda engine management, specific models utilize hardware from alternative suppliers.

*   **Keihin IPT:** Primary supplier for the majority of Honda/Acura engine management systems.
*   **Motorola:** Documented as the manufacturer for specific powertrain control modules, such as the 1998 Honda Accord ECM/PCM.
