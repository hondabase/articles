# P04

The P04 came in European cars. I'm not sure which years or models, so someone fill this in. They look nearly identical in design to **late model** [PM5](/cars/rom/pm5) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s.

- 1992 Civic 1.5 LSi with a d15b2 engine [DPFI](/cars/sensors/dpfi) and a P04 [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) ( 37820 P04 G00 )
- 1993 Civic 1.5 LSi with a D15B2 engine [DPFI](/cars/sensors/dpfi) and P04 [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) ( 37820 P04 G02 )
- 1995 Civic 1.5 LSi with a d15b2 engine and a P04 [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) (37820 P04 G04)
