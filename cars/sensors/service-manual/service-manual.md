# Factory Service Manuals (Helms)

Factory Service Manuals (FSMs), frequently referred to as "Helms manuals," are the definitive technical resource for Honda and Acura vehicle repair. These manuals provide comprehensive information regarding mechanical assembly, electrical schematics, and diagnostic troubleshooting procedures.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always prioritize the factory service manual over aftermarket repair guides (e.g., Chilton or Haynes). Factory manuals contain specific wire colors, pinout configurations, and torque specifications essential for accurate diagnostics and repair.

## Manual Contents
A standard factory service manual includes the following sections:

*   **General Information:** Safety precautions, vehicle identification, and maintenance schedules.
*   **Engine/Transmission:** Detailed teardown, rebuild, and inspection procedures.
*   **Electrical:** Complete wiring diagrams, connector views, and component locations.
*   **Fuel and Emissions:** Diagnostic flowcharts for PGM-FI systems and emission control components.
*   **Body and Chassis:** Suspension, steering, braking, and interior trim removal instructions.

## Diagnostic Utility
The electrical section is the primary reference for engine management diagnostics. When troubleshooting ECU-related issues, the manual provides:

*   **Pinout Diagrams:** Mapping of ECU connectors to specific sensors and actuators.
*   **Voltage Specifications:** Expected values for sensor inputs and output signals.
*   **Continuity Testing:** Procedures for verifying harness integrity between the ECU and engine components.
