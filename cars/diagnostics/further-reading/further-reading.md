# EFI Theory and Engine Management Reference

This document provides a curated list of external resources for those seeking to deepen their understanding of Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) theory, ECU architecture, and engine management systems.

## Industry Resources

*   **[Hondata](http://www.hondata.com):** Extensive technical documentation regarding Honda engine management, focusing on the modification and tuning of factory ECUs.
*   **[DIY-EFI](http://www.diy-efi.org):** A comprehensive archive of EFI theory and development. While originally focused on domestic platforms, it contains a wealth of foundational knowledge applicable to all engine management systems.
*   **[EFI Tuning](http://www.efi-tuning.org):** Technical resources dedicated to the tuning of GM and Bosch-based ECU architectures.
*   **[ECI-Multi](http://www.eci-multi.org):** A technical resource focused on Mitsubishi/DSM engine management systems, serving as a useful reference for comparative EFI architecture.
*   **[SDS EFI](http://www.sdsefi.com):** A commercial vendor of standalone engine management systems featuring a high-quality technical library covering fundamental EFI operation and tuning principles.
*   **[HowStuffWorks](http://www.howstuffworks.com):** An excellent resource for understanding the scientific theory and mechanical operation of automotive systems, featuring detailed diagrams and explanations.

## DIY Engine Management Systems

*   **[MegaSquirt](http://www.bgsoflex.com/megasquirt.html):** A well-documented, open-source fuel injection computer project featuring complete schematics and community-driven development.
*   **[MegaJolt](http://www.bgsoflex.com/mjl/mjl_edis.html):** A specialized system designed for the control of Ford EDIS ignition modules.
*   **[MSExtra](https://www.msextra.com):** Advanced documentation for DIY spark control systems, including integration with external ignition drivers like the MSD 6AL.

> [!NOTE]
> While these resources provide valuable theoretical and practical information, always verify specific pinouts and wiring diagrams against the factory service manual for your specific chassis and engine combination.
