# Further Reading

So you want to learn more about EFI theory, [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s, or just stuff in general. This is intended to be a place to put links to stuff worth reading. [Hon Data](/cars/diagnostics/hon-data)'s [website ](http://www.hondata.com) - tons of good information, much of it very aplicable considering they use stock [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s as the base for their standalone [http://web.archive.org/web/20250925104144/http://web.archive.org/web/20250925104144/http://www.diy-efi.org//](http://web.archive.org/web/20250925104144/http://web.archive.org/web/20250925104144/http://www.diy-efi.org//) - started out with a heavy slant towards domestics but has a WEALTH of good knowledge and tons of ***very*** smart individuals on the mailing list [http://web.archive.org/web/20110910002838/http://web.archive.org/web/20110910002838/http://www.efi-tuning.org//](http://web.archive.org/web/20110910002838/http://web.archive.org/web/20110910002838/http://www.efi-tuning.org//) - a site dedicated to tuning mostly GM and Bosch (BMW,etc.) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s [http://www.eci-multi.org](http://www.eci-multi.org) - PGMFI's equivalent for DSMs. [http://www.sdsefi.com](http://www.sdsefi.com) - a commercial vendor of standalone engine management systems that has a tech section with every article worth reading twice [http://www.howstuffworks.com](http://www.howstuffworks.com) - a great site with many quality articles on automotive related topics, as well as a ton of information on the scientific theory behind the operation of things. Tons of pictures, fascinating redaing. [http://www.bgsoflex.com/megasquirt.html](http://www.bgsoflex.com/megasquirt.html) - The Mega Squirt is a homebrew [DIY](/cars/sensors/diy) fuel injection computer that is pretty well documented and has full schematic. [http://www.bgsoflex.com/mjl/mjl_edis.html](http://www.bgsoflex.com/mjl/mjl_edis.html) - Mega Jolt is a prtially complete system for controlling ford EDIS modules [https://www.msextra.com](https://www.msextra.com) - Mega Spark is a [DIY](/cars/sensors/diy) system for controlling spark standalone using a 6AL or similar to drive the spark itself.
