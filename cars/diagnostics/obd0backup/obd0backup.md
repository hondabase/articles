# OBD0BACKUP

the BACK0004 Processor made by NEC is present in all [OBD0 MPFI](/cars/diagnostics/obd0mpfi) [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu)s. It runs the car when the [CEL](/cars/wiring/cel) is lit solid red and the [ECU](/cars/ecu/ecu) is in [Limp Mode](/cars/diagnostics/limp-mode).
