> [!IMPORTANT]
> Ensure the ECU board revision matches the specific guide before beginning any soldering or component removal. Verify all jumper settings and socket orientations against the provided visual references.

> [!CAUTION]
> Static electricity can damage sensitive ECU components. Always use an anti-static wrist strap and a grounded workstation when performing board-level modifications.
