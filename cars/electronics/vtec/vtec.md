# VTEC

VTEC is honda's patented system for variable [Cam Profile](/cars/electronics/cam-profile)s. It effectively gives an engine two independent cam profiles. The idea is that the [Low Cam](/cars/electronics/low-cam) has conditions optimal for low [RPM](/cars/electronics/rpm)s and the [High Cam](/cars/electronics/high-cam) has conditions optimal for higher [RPM](/cars/electronics/rpm)s. VTEC motors generally have a broader powerband characterized by two "humps" as opposed to one hump with non-vtec motors. The ideal [VTEC Crossover Point](/cars/electronics/vtec-crossover-point) can be found by superimposing a dyno graph of the [Low Cam](/cars/electronics/low-cam) and [High Cam](/cars/electronics/high-cam) and seeing where they cross.
