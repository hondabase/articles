# OBD0BACKUP

the BACK0004 Processor made by NEC is present in all [OBD0 MPFI](/cars/electronics/obd0mpfi) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s. It runs the car when the [CEL](/cars/electronics/cel) is lit solid red and the [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) is in [Limp Mode](/cars/electronics/limp-mode).
