# BAR

One [BAR](/cars/electronics/bar) is equal to 14.7 psi. Also called ""atm"" or ""atmospheres"" because it corresponds to atmospheric pressure at sea level. psi = bar * 14.7 The metric unit is the Pascal (Pa) or Kilo Pascal (KPa) but I'm not 100% sure on the conversions.
