# Prelude Accord

OK, here is what I know about these [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s. The PT6-A51 I have here has a 66911 [MCU](/cars/electronics/mcu) and uses an external 256k PROM. I got an [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) out of a 1994 Prelude SI with a 1.6 [DOHC](/cars/electronics/dohc) and it has a PK3-A00 and it also has the OKI 66911 [MCU](/cars/electronics/mcu) and external 256Kbit PROM, plus has the `74HC373` -- sloopy - 16 May 2005
