# Electronic Advance

[Electronic Advance](/cars/electronics/electronic-advance) refers to a motor setup where [Spark Advance](/cars/electronics/spark-advance) can be controlled electronically by the [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu). Electronic Advance replaced [Vacuum Advance](/cars/electronics/vacuum-advance) because it is much more flexible for tuning, and allows much better control of the motor. Distributorless direct-fire distributorless ignitions are largely replacing electronic advance now (early 2004).
