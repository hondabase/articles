# Combustion Chamber

the combustion chamber is an area of the Cylinder Head where the [Spark Plug](/cars/electronics/spark-plug) sits to ignite air and fuel. Burning of air and fuel occurs here. The shape of the combustion chamber is one of the factors that can shape how easily [Pre Ignition](/cars/electronics/pre-ignition) occurs.
