# Saturated Injectors

[Saturated Injectors](/cars/electronics/saturated-injectors) aka [High Impedance Injectors](/cars/electronics/high-impedance-injectors)Impedance typically 12-16ohms. At 12volts, this is a draw of ~1-.75 amps. The low current helps keep the injectors cool. The saturated injectors see a steady 12volts, and ground is modulated to produce pulsing. These are the stock style of injector for [OBD1](/cars/electronics/obd1) and up hondas, so no resistor box is needed.
