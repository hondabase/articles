# PM9

Essentially a [PM5](/cars/electronics/pm5)The PM5 + PM9 are essentially the same [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu). I think one may have been for the 4 speed and one the 5 speed, but I'm not sure. (blundar) The PM9 that I posted was from a 4 speed HF model Civic. You may be correct in your thoughts. (Warrick) The PM5+PM9 used two distinct designs (kinda like the [PG7](/cars/electronics/pg7)) - the 88-89 Use an `83C154` while the 90-91 use a 66201. The 90-91 processor quadrant looks a lot like an [OBD](/cars/electronics/obd)1 [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu).
