# Vacuum Advance

Vaccuum Advance is a purely mechanical method for varying the amount of [Spark Advance](/cars/electronics/spark-advance) based on engine load. A vac-advance distributor is connected to a source of engine vacuum. As vacuum increases, forces of suction rotate the distributor, creating more ignition advance. **The [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) cannot control timing at all with a vacuum advance distributor.**
