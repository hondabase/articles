# JDM

Japanese Domestic Market - cars produced for use in Japan Compare to: [EDM](/cars/electronics/edm), [USDM](/cars/electronics/usdm)Note: [JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm) [OBD1 Civic Integra](/cars/electronics/obd1-civic-integra) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s generally lack the [Electrical Load Detector](/cars/electronics/electrical-load-detector) and [Pressure Atmosphere](/cars/electronics/pressure-atmosphere) sensors (compared to [USDM](/cars/electronics/usdm) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s). They generally have more surface mount components, and use a smaller square case. They also generally have a ~112mph [Speed Limiter](/cars/electronics/speed-limiter).
