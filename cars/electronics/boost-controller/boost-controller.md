# Boost Controller

A boost controller is a device that allows the levels of boost generated by a turbo to be controlled. ***NOTE***: Boost controllers can only ***raise*** boost levels above the level set by your [Waste Gate](/cars/electronics/waste-gate)! See also [Electronic Boost Controller](/cars/electronics/electronic-boost-controller) and [ECUBoost Controller](/cars/electronics/ecu-boost-controller)
