# PS9 Auto Manual

Remove R62,R64,R66 right by the center divider to turn an auto [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) into a 5 spd. [JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s also require the addition of BR3. This also applies to PG7, PM6 and PM7 [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s which share the same pc board.
