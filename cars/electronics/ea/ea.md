# EA

_EA (EA Prime) is a pin on the [MCU](/cars/electronics/mcu) that controls whether the [MCU](/cars/electronics/mcu) runs off [Internal ROM](/cars/electronics/internal-rom) or an external [ROM](/cars/electronics/rom) chip. _EA is pin 31 on 40-pin [Intel8051](/cars/electronics/intel8051) [MCU](/cars/electronics/mcu)s [J1 ](/cars/electronics/obd1j1) on [OBD1](/cars/electronics/obd1) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s controls the state of _EA, and is used to activate an external [ROM](/cars/electronics/rom) you install.
