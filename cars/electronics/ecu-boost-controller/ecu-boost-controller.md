# ECU Boost Controller

The [Boost Controller](/cars/electronics/boost-controller) feature is a DEVELOPMENTAL feature where the [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) controls boost by sending a [PWM](/cars/electronics/pwm) signal to a bleed solenoid on the wastegate pressure line, just like an [Electronic Boost Controller](/cars/electronics/electronic-boost-controller) would.
