# Boost Cut

Boost Cut refers to a [Fuel Cut](/cars/electronics/fuel-cut) or [Spark Cut](/cars/electronics/spark-cut) that is triggered to prevent the motor from damage from too much [Boost](/cars/electronics/boost).
