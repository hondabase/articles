# Spark Cut

A spark cut is when fuel continues to be supplied to the motor but no sparks are triggered to ignite the fuel. This is a good way to shoot flames out of the back of a hot exhaust. [Spark Cut](/cars/electronics/spark-cut)s are generally implemented by aftermarket ignition systems. The stock [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) uses a [Fuel Cut](/cars/electronics/fuel-cut) for [Rev Limiter](/cars/electronics/rev-limiter)s and [Speed Limiter](/cars/electronics/speed-limiter)s.
