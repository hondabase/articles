# EPROM

Electrically Programmable [ROM](/cars/electronics/rom)[ROM](/cars/electronics/rom) that can be programmed using a [Rom Burner](/cars/electronics/rom-burner)The most common types pin-compatible with most [OBD0](/cars/electronics/obd0) and [OBD1](/cars/electronics/obd1) Honda [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s are the [27 C256](/cars/electronics/27c256) and the [29 C256](/cars/electronics/29c256). Note, the [28 C256](/cars/electronics/28c256) is not pin-compatible.
