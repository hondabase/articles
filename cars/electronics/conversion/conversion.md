# Conversion

Describe [Conversion](/cars/electronics/conversion) here. Conversion is the act of modifying the [Wire Harness](/cars/electronics/wire-harness) at the [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) so you can fit ohter Generation ([OBD](/cars/electronics/obd)) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu). For example, you might want to run an [OBD1](/cars/electronics/obd1) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) into a factory [OBD2](/cars/electronics/obd2) car.
