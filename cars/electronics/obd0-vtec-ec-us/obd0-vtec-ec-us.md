# OBD0 VTEC EC Us

The [OBD0](/cars/electronics/obd0) VTEC [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)'s are the PR3 and the PW0. These [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s ran the B16A1 in [OBD0](/cars/electronics/obd0) cars from 89-91. There is no code interchangeability between the two [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)'s. They use a crystal that operates at 7.900 MHZ These [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s presently arent getting much love from the development folks.
