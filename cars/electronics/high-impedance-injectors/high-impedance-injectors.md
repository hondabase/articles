# High Impedance Injectors

High-impedance injectors, also called saturated injectors, have a relatively high coil
resistance (roughly 12 ohms and up). The ECU can switch them directly with a simple
saturated driver, so they need no resistor pack or special driver. Most factory Honda
injectors are high-impedance, which is why they plug straight into the stock harness.

They are the easy choice for most street and mild builds: simpler wiring, less heat, and
direct compatibility with stock and most aftermarket ECUs. Only very large, high-flow
injectors are commonly offered as [low-impedance](/cars/electronics/low-impedance-injectors)
units instead.
