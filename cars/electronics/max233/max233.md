# `MAX233`

the [Maxim ](http://www.maxim-ic.com) `MAX233` chip is commonly used to translate between [TTL](/cars/electronics/ttl) and [RS232](/cars/electronics/rs232) levels during [serial communication](/cars/electronics/serial-communication). It does not require any external capacitors, unlike the `MAX232`. [Datasheet here](http://pdfserv.maxim-ic.com/en/ds/MAX220-MAX249.pdf).
