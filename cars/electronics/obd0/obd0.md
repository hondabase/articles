# OBD0

"OBD0" is not an official standard like [OBD2](/cars/electronics/obd2). It is most usually used to refer to [MPFI](/cars/electronics/mpfi) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s made from 88-91 with [Electronic Advance Distributors](/cars/electronics/electronic-advance-distributors) found on [OBD0 MPFI](/cars/electronics/obd0mpfi) cars of this era. Technically, all [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s made prior to 1988 and all accord/prelude/v6/[OBD0 VTEC ECUs](/cars/electronics/obd0-vtec-ec-us) of this era are also "OBD0" but due to the drastic differences, they are grouped seperately and referred to as "pre-OBD" [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s, or another term. [OBD](/cars/electronics/obd)0 [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s (88-91)

- [PG7](/cars/electronics/pg7) : 88-89 Integra (D16A1)
- [PM6](/cars/electronics/pm6) : 88-91 Civic/CRX [SOHC](/cars/electronics/sohc) Si (1.6l SOHC)
- [PM7](/cars/electronics/pm7) : 89-91 Civic Si [DOHC](/cars/electronics/dohc) ZC ([JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm) EF3)
- [PM7](/cars/electronics/pm7) : 89-91 CRX D16A8 , D16A9 ( [EDM](/cars/electronics/edm) )
- [PM8](/cars/electronics/pm8) : 88-91 CRX HF
- [PR3](/cars/electronics/pr3) : 89-91 [JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm) B16A EF8/9
- [PR5](/cars/electronics/pr5) : 90-91 [JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm) Integra ZXi (1.6 SOHC)
- [PW0](/cars/electronics/pw0) : 89-91 [JDM](/cars/electronics/jdm)/Euro B16A EF8/9 DA6-XSi ([DOHC](/cars/electronics/dohc) VTEC)
- [PR4](/cars/electronics/pr4) : 90-91 Integra LS/GS (b18a)
- [PS9](/cars/electronics/ps9) : 88-91 4 door civic EX Auto (SOHC 1.6)

 The PH3 and PK3 [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu) shares almost the same PC board. - [PK2](/cars/electronics/pk2) : 88-91 Prelude Si B20A engine [DOHC](/cars/electronics/dohc) 140 HP
- [PH3](/cars/electronics/ph3) : 86-89 Accord 2.0Si B20A

These [DPFI](/cars/electronics/dpfi) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s were also made during the same time period: - [PM5](/cars/electronics/pm5) : 88-91 Civic/CRX DX ([DPFI](/cars/electronics/dpfi) D15b)
- [PM9](/cars/electronics/pm9) : 88-91 Civic base model (DPFID15b)
