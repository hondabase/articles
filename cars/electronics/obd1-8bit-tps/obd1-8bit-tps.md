# OBD1 8bit TPS

If x is a 8-bit TPS value, then:

- TPS(''x'') = ((hextodec''x'')/229.5*100)''%''

[WOT](/cars/electronics/wot)(100%)= 4.5 v = 4.5/5*255 = 229.5 by Calvin Baank
