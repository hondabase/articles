# Fuel Octane

Fuel "Octane" is a measure of gasoline's resistance to [Pre Ignition](/cars/electronics/pre-ignition). the [Gasoline FAQ ](http://www.faqs.org/faqs/autos/gasoline-faq/) explains all you ever wanted to know about gasoline, including a very good section on how octane is measured, what octane numbers mean and what you can do to increase the octane rating of gasoline.
