# VTEC Crossover Point

In a [VTEC](/cars/electronics/vtec) motor, the [VTEC Crossover Point](/cars/electronics/vtec-crossover-point) is the [RPM](/cars/electronics/rpm) at which the [VTEC Solenoid](/cars/electronics/vtec-solenoid) is activated in order to switch from the [Low Cam](/cars/electronics/low-cam) to the [High Cam](/cars/electronics/high-cam).
