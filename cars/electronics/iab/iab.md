# IAB

Intake Air Butterflies - a valve that opens and closes to switch between two available passages in the intake manifold. This is done to allow the use of a longer runner at low [RPM](/cars/electronics/rpm)s for increased torque and a short runner at high [RPM](/cars/electronics/rpm)s for more power. Found on GSR and H22A intake manifolds.
