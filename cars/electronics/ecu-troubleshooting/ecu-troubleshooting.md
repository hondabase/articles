# Ecu Troubleshooting

"my chipped [ECU](/pgmfi/wiki/library/ecu) doesn't work! Help!" When you say "it doesn't work," what do you mean? There are a couple of common problems encountered when chipping [ECU](/pgmfi/wiki/library/ecu)s:

- Sometimes the [CEL](/pgmfi/wiki/library/cel) will come on and there will be a pattern of flashes indicating an error code present. See [Checking Error Codes](/pgmfi/wiki/library/checking-error-codes).
- Sometimes the [CEL](/pgmfi/wiki/library/cel) will come on and just stay on continuously. Attempts to jump the service check connector to check for code blinks fail. See [Troubleshooting Solid CEL](/pgmfi/wiki/library/troubleshooting-solid-cel).
- Sometimes the [CEL](/pgmfi/wiki/library/cel) is not on, but the car still runs horribly. See Troubleshooting Operation
