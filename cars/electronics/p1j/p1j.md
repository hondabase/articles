# P1J

P1J and P1K come from 96-00 UK civics with D14 engines. Despite the years, those are OBDI [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s and seem to be easyly convertable for VTEC Sorry for the bad images, please consult the page on [P1 K](/cars/electronics/p1k) for better views of the [PCB](/cars/electronics/pcb). Those OBDI P1J or P1K [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s share a strange board layout labeled as: "2PU6098-4460P1 5 A8E-A3", but get easyly chipped. VTEC converion seems easy, but is not yet tested.
