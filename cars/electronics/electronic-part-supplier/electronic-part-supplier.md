# Electronic Part Supplier

a handful of major electronic part suppliers:

- [Digikey](http://www.digikey.com) - good in-stock availability, great catalog, good website.
- [Arrow](http://www.arrow.com) - GREAT in-stock availability, and they now carry AT29C256 Flash PROMs, $5 ea, no minimum. (12/03)
- [All American Semiconductor](http://www.allamerican.com) - Good place for AT[29 C256](/cars/electronics/29c256) but $50 minimum order.
- [Newark](http://www.newark.com) - fast, professional shipping
- [NuHorizons](http://www.nuhorizons.com) - I ordered the groupbuy of 500 SI-5151SG [515 X High Side Switch](/cars/electronics/515x-high-side-switch)es from them.
- [MOATES.NET](http://www.moates.net) - from DIY-EFI.ORG Craig offers lots of help and great support. $6 29c256, $7 lowpro [ZIF](/cars/electronics/zif), $6 shipping.
- [ProgShop](http://progshop.com/shop/eproms/) - Germany location, so shipping is expensive to U.S., but it's another resource...
