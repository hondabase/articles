# Non Vtec-To-Vtec

In most cases, it is relatively simple to convert non-vtec [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s to VTEC. The same [PCB](/cars/electronics/pcb) was used in several [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s, with components added or left off as needed. Look for a part number like "02D011F0-1500" which was used in many 94-95 Civic [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s. In many cases, you might need to look at [One Wire To4 Wire O2 Sensor](/cars/electronics/one-wire-to4-wire-o2-sensor) or [Auto To Manual](/cars/electronics/auto-to-manual) as well. Conversion for [02 D01720-1500](/cars/electronics/02d01720-1500) boards Conversion for [02 D011 F0-1500](/cars/electronics/02d011f0-1500) boards Conversion for [02 D01980-1500](/cars/electronics/02d01980-1500) boards Conversion for [P14 to P13](/cars/electronics/p14-to-p13) (Which board?) Use [5050s](/cars/electronics/5050s) as a VTEC driver for the above conversion
