# Ac Switch

You have to jump the A/C Thermostat switch under the dash it is a 2P connector blue taped to the harness by the heater blower area. Wire colors are blue/red to yellow/white. Under hood there is the A/C Subharness connector on the passenger side you need to jump blue/red yellow/white. I will try to get pics tommorow.
