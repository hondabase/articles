# PCB

[PCB](/cars/electronics/pcb) stands for printed circuit boards. [PCB](/cars/electronics/pcb)s are the boards [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s and computers in general use. They are made by printing a design onto a copper plated board and then etching it to leave a design of wires and pads. You can either make them yourself or send away for them to be professionally manufactured.
