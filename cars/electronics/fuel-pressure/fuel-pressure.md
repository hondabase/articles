# Fuel Pressure

You can increase the amount of fuel delivered by increasing your static [Fuel Pressure](/cars/electronics/fuel-pressure). High [Fuel Pressure](/cars/electronics/fuel-pressure) may however slow the injector from opening, sacrificing crisp fuel delivery. Honda OEM fule pressure should be 38-42psi.
