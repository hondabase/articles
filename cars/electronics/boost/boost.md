# Boost

"Boost" refers to when the pressure inside an engine's intake manifold is greater than [Atmospheric Pressure](/cars/electronics/atmospheric-pressure). Remember, 1psi of boost is ***not*** 1psi of [Absolute Pressure](/cars/electronics/absolute-pressure)!
