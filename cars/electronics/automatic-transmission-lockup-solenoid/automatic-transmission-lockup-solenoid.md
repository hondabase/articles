# Automatic Transmission Lockup Solenoid

the Automatic Transmission Lockup Solenoid is a solenoid present in automatic cars transmissions that causes the torque converter to lock under control of the [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu). It is not needed on manual transmission cars, obviously, so it is frequently hijacked to control other things. It is pin A8 on [OBD0](/cars/electronics/obd0) [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s.
