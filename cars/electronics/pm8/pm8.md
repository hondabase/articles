# PM8

The PM8 came in the US on 88-91 CRX HF. These [ECU](/cars/electronics/ecu)s always lacked external [ROM](/cars/electronics/rom)s, and they seem to be just a wee lil bit different than [PM6](/cars/electronics/pm6)/[PM7](/cars/electronics/pm7) in terms of hardware configuration. They are much more similar to the [PM6](/cars/electronics/pm6)/[PM7](/cars/electronics/pm7) than the [PR4](/cars/electronics/pr4)-- [The Admin](/pgmfi/wiki/home/the-admin) - 18 Dec 2003
