# Ecu Sensors

Sensors present on almost all honda motors:

- [Crankshaft Position Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/crankshaft-position-sensor) - Determines timing for fuel injection and ignition of each cylinder and also detects engine speed.
- [Top Dead Center Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/top-dead-center-sensor) - Determines ignition timing at start-up (cranking) and when crank angle is abnormal.
- [Cylinder Position Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/cylinder-position-sensor) - Detects position of #1 cylinder for sequential spark ignition to each cylinder.
- [Engine Coolant Temperature](/pgmfi/wiki/library/engine-coolant-temperature) - or ''ECT'' thermocouple input for engine coolant temperature
- [Intake Air Temperature Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/intake-air-temperature-sensor) - or ''IAT'' thermocouple input for intake air temperature
- [Map Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/map-sensor) - Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor, allows [ECU](/pgmfi/wiki/library/ecu) to adjust [Air Fuel Ratio](/pgmfi/wiki/library/air-fuel-ratio) based on density of intake air
- [Oxygen Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/oxygen-sensor) - Measures oxygen content of exhaust gas
- [Pressure Atmosphere](/pgmfi/wiki/library/pressure-atmosphere) - or ''PA'' or ''Barometric Pressure Sensor'' measures atmospheric pressure.
- [Tps Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/tps-sensor) - Throttle Position Sensor
- [Vehicle Speed Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/vehicle-speed-sensor) - or ''VSS'' measures how fast ***axles*** are turning
- [Idle Air Control Valve](/pgmfi/wiki/library/idle-air-control-valve) / [Electronic Air Control Vale](/pgmfi/wiki/library/electronic-air-control-vale) - Or ''IAC/EACV'' Helps control idle

Optional sensors: - [Electrical Load Detector](/pgmfi/wiki/library/electrical-load-detector) - or ''ELD'' present in most US cars to measure how much electrical load there is
- [Knock Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/knock-sensor) - A microphone-like device to listen for [Pre Ignition](/pgmfi/wiki/library/pre-ignition), or knocking in [DOHC](/pgmfi/wiki/library/dohc) [Vtec](/pgmfi/wiki/library/vtec) motors.
- [Pa Sensor](/pgmfi/wiki/library/pa-sensor) - Atmospheric Pressure sensor
- [Vtec Solenoid](/pgmfi/wiki/library/vtec-solenoid) = solenoid to route oil flow to activate [Vtec](/pgmfi/wiki/library/vtec) (on Vtec motors only :) )
